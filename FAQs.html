Frequently Asked Questions?

A portfolio is a curated collection of work samples, projects, or achievements that showcase an individual's skills, abilities, and accomplishments. In the context of a professional or creative career, a portfolio serves as a visual representation of a person's capabilities and expertise.

A portfolio showcases your skills, experiences, and projects to potential employers or clients. It provides a visual representation of your work and demonstrates your expertise in a particular field.

Your portfolio should include experience, a variety of projects that highlight your skills. This may include examples of web design, graphics, illustrations, etc. Achievements, soft skills, position of responsibilities, extra-curricular activities. And much more as per your interests.

Regular updates are crucial to reflect your latest work and skills. Aim to update your portfolio at least every few months, or whenever you complete a significant project.

Your portfolio is a powerful tool for attracting potential employers or clients. It allows them to assess your skills, style, and versatility, making it easier for them to visualize how you can contribute to their projects.
